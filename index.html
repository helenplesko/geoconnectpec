GeoConnect Prince Edward County: Strengthening Rural Connections through Mapping

About the Project

Description of Project

This project involves the use of Esri products to effectively display community-driven data to expand The County Foundation’s Vital Signs program's existing resources and aid in decision-making in housing and public transit for Prince Edward County.

Project Objectives

This project aims to:

  1. Provide client with software solutions and community data
  2. Enhance geographic data analysis and visualization for effective decision-making relating to housing and public transit​
  3. Maximize GIS efficiency through user role definition, metadata creation, training, and ArcGIS Hub establishment

Study Area

Meet the Client

The County Foundation

The County Foundation is a Canadian community foundation located in Prince Edward County, Ontario. They manage three key components of the county: community grants, collaborative action, and Vital Signs data. Through these three areas, The County Foundation has made, and continues to make, incredible contributions to PEC to better the well-being of the community.

Vital Signs

The County Foundation’s Vital Signs program aims to improve the quality of life and increase the sense of belonging of those living and visiting PEC through analysis of community-driven data. It provides reports, summaries, resources, and data for the public based on nine key themes: community, economy, education, environment, food security, health, housing, safety, and transportation.
